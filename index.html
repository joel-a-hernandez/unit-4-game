Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to the sum of your crystals.

You win the game by matching the sum of your crystals to the target number, you lose the game if your score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
